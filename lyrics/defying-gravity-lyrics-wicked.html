"Defying Gravity," to Wicked's Elphaba, means she has escaped the "good witch" label she thought she belonged to. But what gives this song a universal connection is the overall idea of letting go of society's expectations and just being yourself. This song, in turn, has not only become a self-esteem anthem, but a gay rights one as well.
