Meaning
by

Dec 11 2015
"The Hills" is the lead single of Beauty Behind the Madness (2015), the second album of Canadian recording artist The Weeknd, a.k.a. Abel Tesfaye.

The song was written by Ahmad Balshe, Carlo Montagnese, Emmanuel Nickerson and Abel Tesfaye himself. It was produced by The Weeknd's long-term collaborator, DJ Carlo "Illangelo" Montagnese and by DJ "Million Dollar" Mano, Kanye West's former tour DJ.

"The Hills" reached No. 1 on the US Billboard Hot 100.
