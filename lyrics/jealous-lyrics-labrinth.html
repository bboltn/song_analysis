The song "Jealous" by British musician Labrinth, a.k.a. Timothy McKenzie, was released on 23 November 2014 as a second single for his album "Take Me To The Truth" (2015). The artist co-wrote the song with Josh Kear and Natalie Hemby and produced it himself.

The song was described as a "heart-breaking ballad" by MTV. It peaked at No. 7 in the UK and No. 5 in Scotland. The lyrics are about Labrinth's father, who abandoned him and his family when he was only 4 years old. Timothy McKenzie and his eight siblings were subsequently raised by their mother Pamela in Hackney, London.…

Labrinth comes from a family with deep musical roots. Josh McKenzie, known by his stage name MckNasty, is a DJ and established drummer. Mac1 is a music producer. His sister Shezar is a singer and backing vocalist. Labrinth has 8 siblings and all his sisters are singers while all his brothers are musicians. Together, they were called Mac 9 while growing up. Labrinth's aunt, Sandra Edwards, was the lead singer of house act The Happy Clappers which reached the UK Top 10 in 1995 with track "I Believe".
