My husband passed in May of 2017. I turned on the radio, by accident when reaching to turn off the car, and again words sent from Larry were playing. The first words I heard were 'the moon and the stars above'....this is far from the first time something like this has happened since he's been on the other side. He was saved, and many times he said similar things to me as are in the song, so thank you for recording it. Karen Viers Howk/Des Moines, IA
