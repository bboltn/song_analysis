"Chandelier" is the lead single from Australian recording artist Sia's sixth studio album, 1000 Forms of Fear (2014). It is her first single in four years. It was written by Sia Furler, Jesse Shatkin and produced by Greg Kurstin and Jesse Shatkin.

"Chandelier" is an electropop song containing melancholic lyrics that explore the struggles of a party girl using her addiction to alcohol as a destructive coping mechanisms in order to deal with her life. Billboard magazine chose this as their Best Song of 2014.
