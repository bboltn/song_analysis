"Elastic Heart" is a song by Sia featuring The Weeknd and American producer Diplo. The song is from the soundtrack for the 2013 American film The Hunger Games: Catching Fire. A solo version of "Elastic Heart" was included on Sia's sixth studio album "1000 Forms of Fear" and re-released as a single in January 2015. The song was written by Sia Furler, Thomas Wesley Pentz, Andrew Swanson and The Weeknd (Abel Tesfaye). It was produced by Diplo and American producer Greg Kurstin.
