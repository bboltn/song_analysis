Meaning
by
AmandaH
, Editor
Feb 01 2016
It took a long time for "Midnight Train To Georgia" to reach Gladys Knight & The Pips. Originally written and recorded by Jim Weatherly as a country song (with 'Houston' instead of 'Georgia'), it eventually reached soul singer Cissy Houston. Houston's version changed it to 'Georgia,' and then Gladys & co. picked it up in 1973. It may be one of the only instances where a song went from country to R&B/soul.
