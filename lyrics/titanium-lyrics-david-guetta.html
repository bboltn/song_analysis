"Titanium" is a song by French DJ and music producer David Guetta, featuring vocals by Australian recording artist Sia. It was released on Guetta's fifth studio album, "Nothing but the Beat" (2011).
The song was written by Sia Furler, David Guetta, Giorgio Tuinfort and Nick Van De Wall (Afrojack), with production also by Guetta,Tuinfort and Afrojack.
