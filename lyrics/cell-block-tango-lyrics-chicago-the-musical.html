Hunyak's entire monologue is completely removed from this, but I believe it can still be sent in.
She asks how she ever got here, to the Cook County Jail. She recounts how "they" (most likely the police) say that her lover held down her husband while she decapitated him, and she tries to explain that she's innocent. She says "I don't understand why Uncle Sam says I did it," Uncle Sam most likely meaning the police. She then goes on to say "I tried to explain down at the police station, but they couldn't understand me!", prompting Roxie to ask if she actually did it. "Uh-uh. Not. Guilty!"
